Stores

Taniti’s grocery scene is designed to meet the needs of both locals and tourists.

The island has two well-stocked supermarkets that offer a wide variety of fresh produce, dairy, meats, canned goods, and international products.

In addition, two smaller grocery stores cater to those looking for quick essentials or specialty items, often with a focus on local ingredients like tropical fruits, fish, and coconut products.

For convenience, there’s a 24-hour store, perfect for late-night snacks or last-minute necessities.

These stores provide a mix of both imported and island-grown goods, making it easy for visitors to stock up on supplies or sample local flavors during their stay.

  • Taniti Market

    Taniti Market is one of the island’s main supermarkets, offering a wide selection of fresh produce, dairy, meats, and international products.
    It’s the perfect spot for stocking up on essentials, with a focus on both imported goods and locally sourced items, providing everything visitors need for their stay.
